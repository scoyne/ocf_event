About Our Festival

WELCOME BACK TO THE OHIO CELTIC FESTIVAL

Dear Ohio Celtic Festival Family,
Mark your calendars and dust off your dancing shoes because we're thrilled to announce the triumphant return of the Ohio Celtic Festival! Join us for a weekend filled with merriment, music, and memories, as we celebrate the richness of Irish and Celtic culture at the Lake County Fairgrounds in Painesville Township, Ohio from August 9th to 11th, 2024.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to each and every one of you who has supported us throughout the years. Your enthusiasm and dedication have made the Ohio Celtic Festival a beacon of joy and community spirit. Without your unwaivering support, this wouldn't be possible.

As we gear up for this spectacular event, we invite you, our cherished followers and supporters, to come together once again. Let's create new memories, share laughter, and revel in the beauty of Irish and Celtic Heritage.

What to expect:

  • Live Celtic music that will set your soul dancing
  • Traditional Irish dance performances that will leave you in awe
  • Mouthwatering food that will satisify your cravings
  • Unique vendors offering treasures inspired by Celtic craftsmanship
  • ...And much more!
So, mark your calendars, gather your friends and family and join us for a weekend filled with joy, camaraderie, and the magic of Celtic traditions.

Thank you for being a part of our incredible journey. We can't wait to welcome you back to the Ohio Celtic Festival - where the spirit of the Celts comes alive!

Cheers to a fantastic reunion!

Lake County Fairgrounds

Festival location, information, and gallery

Lake County Fairgrounds, Painesville, Ohio

The Lake County Fairgrounds is located at 1301 Mentor Ave, Painesville Township, Ohio 44077. The Lake County Fairgrounds offers a delightful, family-friendly atmosphere, perfect for a day of fun and entertainment. The facilities are well-maintained with restrooms conveniently located throughout the grounds ensuring a comfortable experience for visitors of all ages. Friends and Families can enjoy the unique experience of vibrant Irish entertainment, featuring lively music, traditional irish dances, irish food, and bagpipe performances that captivate audiences.
